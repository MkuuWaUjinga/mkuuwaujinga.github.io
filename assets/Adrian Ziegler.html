Adrian Ziegler Blog C'est moi Hi there, I am Adrian :wave:. I am currently doing my Masters in computer science and mathematics at TUM and an honors in technology management at CDTM. My goal is to contribute towards a future where artificial intelligence turned out as good for humanity. This drives my interest in research and product management. As a researcher, I am interested in data-efficient learning, representation learning and new learning paradigms such as self-supervision or causal inference. Progress in these fields would open up doors to new application areas where (labelled) data is hard to obtain. I am also intrigued by interpretable, robust and fair machine learning. Check out some recent projects of mine applying self-supervised learning in computer vision or reinforcement learning . Recently, I also started contributing to Garage to make reinforcement learning more reproducible. During my time at HPI, I built an exploration software for knowledge graphs and a window manager for the Squeak VM. I like to go running and to cycle in my free time :runner:. The day I figure out how to swim fast enough, I might try a triathlon. Playing the piano is a form of meditation for me :notes:.
